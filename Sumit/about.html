About Me

Hi, I’m Sumit Pathak, currently pursuing a B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Chandigarh Engineering College. I’m a tech enthusiast who’s always been fascinated by the way AI and computer vision can transform everyday life—especially in fields like healthcare and immersive technologies.

I believe in using technology to solve real-world problems. One of my most exciting projects is the BioVision Sensor, which integrates ECG, PPG, and skin temperature tracking into VR headsets—bringing health monitoring into immersive experiences. I’ve also developed an OCR and Translation Application that uses Tesseract OCR, Google Translate, and text-to-speech to help users easily extract, translate, and read text from images and PDFs.

Beyond development, I’ve stepped into leadership roles as well. I had the opportunity to manage the Advanced Digital Training & Career Leadership Mentorship Program, supported by ACIC RISE and NITI Aayog. It gave me a chance to blend my coordination skills with my passion for tech education.

My internship at IBM SkillsBuild, in collaboration with AICTE, helped me strengthen my front-end development skills using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—complementing my backend and Python expertise.

With a strong grasp of Python, Django, MySQL, and web technologies, I’m constantly learning and exploring. I’ve earned certifications from AWS, IBM, Microsoft, and more. I also enjoy participating in hackathons and tech events, recently securing 2nd place in a technical riddle competition.

Whether I’m coding, coordinating, or collaborating—I’m always focused on building something meaningful.
